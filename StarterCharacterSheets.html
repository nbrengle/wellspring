Starter Character Sheets

Not sure where to start when building a character? The following are a series of build “archetypes” to get you started. They are playable as is, but you can also customize them further by changing their Lineage, adding Flaws, or choosing different Powers or Spells. Then all you need to do is give them a name and a backstory to make them your own!
